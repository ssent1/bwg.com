Geotextiles

Geotextile fabric products have many names. You may hear: landscape fabric, non-woven or woven geotextile fabric, geomembrane, geosynthetics, geosynths, and many more. Regardless of the name, every geotextile fabric product has a simple goal: creating harmony with the powerful forces of nature to ensure that your projects stand the test of time while looking as good as the day you finished.

Woven Geotextile Fabrics and Landscape Fabrics

Woven Slit Film | Heavy Weight Woven | Monofilament

Woven geotextile fabric products are stronger than non-woven fabrics. They are ideal for use in situations where resistance to stretching and maintaining the fabric's original shape is important.

Woven geotextile fabrics have outstanding load distribution and water filtration capacity.

Woven geotextile fabrics separate soft soils and harder aggregate granular base materials. They extend the life of roadways by reducing rutting.

Another benefit of using heavy-duty fabrics is that they save significant financial capital by reducing the thickness of the sub-base required.
