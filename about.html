About

BW Geotextile is the best source for Geotextile and Erosion Control Fabric in Kitchener-Waterloo, Cambridge, Guelph and Southwestern Ontario. We manufacture and distribute a full range of silt fence, woven and non-woven geotextile fabrics, and landscape stakes.

BW Geotextile | For Geotextile and Erosion Control Fabrics!

We would like to take this opportunity to thank you for considering BW Geotextile for your geotextile and erosion control fabrics needs. We manufacture and distribute locally a complete range of geotextile and erosion control fabrics in Kitchener-Waterloo, Cambridge, Guelph and Southwestern Ontario since 1989. We specialize in Silt fence, geotextile fabric and geogrids.

To help you manage to minimize sediment erosion control costs on your job sites, you can choose from a variety of contractor grade silt fence. For projects with more challenging requirements, you can choose Ontario Ministry of Transportation (MTO) approved light and heavy duty silt fence options. Wire backed silt fence is an excellent option for projects requiring additional reinforcement. Please note that we can custom stencil your business name, logo, phone number, etc. on your silt fence.

You can address a wide range of application with our non-woven and woven geotextile fabrics. We carry Typar™ landscape fabrics in a variety of convenient sizes. To learn more about specific products, please visit our Products section.
