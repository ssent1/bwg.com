  • Silt Fence: Erosion Control for Job Sites

    Silt fences are suitable for perimeter control, placed below areas where sheet flows discharge from the site. Silt fences should also be used as interior controls below disturbed areas where runoff may occur.

    • Contractor grade silt fence.
    • Ontario Ministry of Transportation (MTO) silt fence.
    • Wire backed silt fence.
    Learn More: Silt Fence

  • Geotextile Fabric: Drainage, Filtration, and Load Distribution

    Non-woven geotextile fabric offers outstanding physical and hydraulic properties in addition to high tensile strengths. Woven geotextile fabric is produced for its high tensile strength and superior load distribution and filtration characteristics.

    • Light to heavy-duty non-woven geotextile and geomembrane fabric.
    • Soft soil/granular base woven geotextile and geomembrane fabric separators.
    • Typar® landscape geotextile fabric for weed control and top soil/subgrade soil separation

  • Geogrid: Slope and Base Reinforcement

    Geogrids are usually used to support and improve the performance of weak subgrades by distributing dynamic loads over a wider surface area. Geogrids for slope reinforcement, retaining walls and embankments are typically coated and impregnated with PVC. Base reinforcement geogrids are ideal for paved and unpaved roads, parking lots and haul roads.

    • Advantex™ Geogrid light- to heavy-weight grids.
    • 4', 6' and 12' geogrids are available for retaining wall applications.
    • Compatible with rectangular gabion basket retaining wall systems.
    Learn More: Geogrids

  • Stakes: Premium Grade Spruce

    All grade stakes, survey stakes, tree stakes, landscaping stakes, and forming stakes are made from premium grade spruce lumber. 1" X 2" stakes are available in 50-stake bundles. 2" X 2" stakes are 25-stake bundles. Custom stakes with painted ends are available.

    • 2" X 2" stakes have a uniform four-sided point.
    • Stake sizes are available from 12" to 96".
    • Tree stakes can be pre-drilled for tie back application.
    Learn More: Landscaping Stakes
